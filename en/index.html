Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can I check in early or check out late?

Early check-in is free and subject to availability. Just let us know when you'll arrive. Check-out is at 11am, sometimes possible until 1pm if occupancy allows. Ask the evening before.

Arriving by car?

Ghent has a low emission zone (LEZ). Foreign vehicles must be registered in advance to avoid a fine. Our hotel and the route to and from the motorway are outside the LEZ, but if you drive into the city centre you will enter it.

Is breakfast included?

Not by default, unless included at booking. No need to reserve in advance. Simply come down when you're ready in the morning, we'll add it to your bill at check-out.

Extra bed or cot?

An extra bed is available at €25 per night. A cot is always free of charge. Just let us know in advance so everything is ready on arrival.

What payment methods are accepted?

Bancontact, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, iDEAL and cash. Feel free to ask about options at booking or arrival.

Until when can I cancel for free?

Until 3 PM the day before arrival with a standard rate. Non-refundable rates cannot be cancelled. See our cancellation policy for full details.

Is smoking allowed?

Smoking is not permitted inside the hotel. There is a designated smoking area at the exit.

Is there parking?

Yes, we have a limited number of parking spaces (€18/night). Please reserve in advance by email, phone or WhatsApp.
