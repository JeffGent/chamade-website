Parking

Several options to park your car safely during your stay.

€18 per night

Our private parking is accessible with your room keycard. The gate closes automatically after three minutes.

Spaces are limited, please let us know in advance if you'd like to reserve. You can do this via the front desk or when booking.

Around the hotel, paid parking applies between 9:00 and 19:00, at €2.20 per hour (max. 5 hours).

On Sundays and public holidays, parking is free.
