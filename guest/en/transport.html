Getting to the centre

The centre of Ghent is about 2 kilometres away. By tram you'll be there in 12 minutes, on foot it's a pleasant half-hour stroll along beautiful avenues.

A 3-minute walk from the hotel brings you to Ghent-Sint-Pieters station. The tram stop is to the right of the main entrance.

Which tram?

Take tram T1 or T3 towards Gentbrugge. Get off at Koophandelsplein (Zonnestraat) or Vogelmarkt, you'll be there in about 12 minutes.

€3 per ride · valid for 1 hour
Buy your ticket at the machines in the station, or pay contactless on board via the white payment terminals.

We're happy to arrange a taxi for you at the front desk. You can also call directly:

  • V-tax: +32 (0)9 222 22 22
  • Taxi Gent: +32 (0)9 333 33 33

You can also request a ride via Uber, which is available in Ghent.
